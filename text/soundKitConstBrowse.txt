You are now browsing the sound pool.

Press shift and the up or down arrows to scroll through the samples in the sound pool. 

Press shift and left arrow to hear audio playback as you scroll. Press shift and the right arrow to hear the file name as you scroll.

Press shift, option, and the number 1 through 8 to place a sample in a slot of the current sound kit. 

Press option and the number 1 through 8 to preview the sample in that slot in the current sound kit.

Press control, option, and the number 1 through 8 to clear that slot in the current sound kit.
